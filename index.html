About Me

I'm currently a senior at the University of Rochester, pursuing a degree in Business with a minor in Computer Science. I have experience in object-oriented programming with languages such as Java and Swift, as well ass experience in analytical scripting using languages such as Python and R. I've also done some work on data visualizations using d3.js and Tableau. My professional interests lie primarily in the fields of Software Development and Data Science, with a connected interest in tech consulting for the Federal Government.

My technical coursework has provided me with not only basic knowledge of several programming tools, but also the ability to pick up new technical skills quickly and with relative ease. At the same time, my business coursework has helped me develop my skills in conveying ideas efficiently. These two channels combine to allow me to be an effective developer who can quickly transition into focusing on the business aspects of a project.
